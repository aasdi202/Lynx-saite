About LYNX

LYNX is a visionary blockchain project designed to revolutionize how we communicate in the decentralized world. By leveraging decentralized identity (DID), LYNX aims to create a secure, private, and censorship-resistant communication ecosystem. Our goal is to empower individuals around the globe to connect, collaborate, and exchange value without intermediaries. LYNX is building the foundation for the future of human interaction — borderless, trustless, and user-controlled.
